Absolut Reader

The next generation e-book reader.
Free, opensource, highly customizable and featuring Chaimager.

  • Download now for android

Dev status

This app in currently in Alpha version, and the first Beta will be released in some weeks from now. Everytime a new version is built you may download it.

Please be aware that bugs might happen, since we are still in Alpha.

But...what is the AbsolutReader and the Chaimager?

Most reading apps out there have some common issues that we want to solve: we want the user to have a 99% customization experience, and a nice and modern looking app without having to pay for this features (like many apps do). Being open-source anyone can join and help coding, and new requested features can be easily added. Our plan is to make the platform available for Android/IOS/Windows so that you may keep reading everytime you want.

Functionalities of AbsolutReader

Some of these are already implemented, others not (marked with *).

Chaimager

Chaimager does not let you forget the characters´ names again! You type in a keyword, and the pdf will be edited so that everytime you click on that name, an information pop up will appear.

Completely free and OpenSource

Anyone is welcome to join our project, and it is completely free to use.

Customizable

Change everything you want, from the background color, to the library style. *

Cloud ready

Never loose your track just because you don´t have your mobile next to you. Keep reading everywhere, since your files can go to the cloud. *

Track your progress

With an account system, check out what your friends are reading, your progress and have full control over your profile. *

Multi platform

Read on your android, IOS, or Windows device! *
