But, what is Absolut Reader?

We are trying to make the next generation e-book reader for all your devices. May you use Windows, Android or IOS, you can access the content and read your favourite books on our app. It is completely free, highly customizable, and brings up Chaimager, so that you will never forget again the name of your characters.

Our self-imposed deadline is having version 1.0 launched before the end of the year, so if you want to join the alpha and beta testing, you can easily download our latest build soon!
Please be aware that many bugs can happen, and we do apologize for them, and ask you to report them on our github page, that will be soon ready for you.

If you are a developer or designer and want to join our team contact us via u/michaelangelito (main head of the project) on reddit. You are always welcome. Currently we are using Django for the server and React-native for the app development, so if you are keen on these topics, message us!

This project is currently on heavy development, and is still not available on the playstore. When beta 0.1 is released, it will be also added. IOS and Windows development is not being develpped for now.
